Q1. Explain the role of operators in javaScript.  Why are they essential in programming?
Ans-
Operators in JavaScript are symbols that perform operations on operands (values or variables). They play a crucial role in programming as they allow developers to manipulate data, perform calculations, and make decisions within their code. Here are some key aspects of the role of operators in JavaScript:

They are essential in programming languages as they can be used to:

1. Perform Arithmetic operations.
2. Assign values using the Assignment operator.
3. Compare values using Comparison Operators.
4. Perform logical operations.
